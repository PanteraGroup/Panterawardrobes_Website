Management

Managing Director

Peter Mills — Managing Director

Peter Mills is the Managing Director of Pantera Wardrobes and has been at the forefront of the business since its inception. With extensive experience in the construction and fit-out industry, Peter oversees all aspects of the business from business development through to delivery.

Robert Farquhar

Commercial Director

Robert Farquhar — Commercial Director

Robert Farquhar is the Commercial Director of Pantera Wardrobes. Robert manages all commercial aspects of the business including estimating, procurement and contract management, ensuring we deliver maximum value to our clients on every project.

Tony Mills

Operations Director

Tony Mills — Operations Director

Tony Mills is the Operations Director of Pantera Wardrobes, responsible for overseeing all operational activity including logistics, warehousing and the management of our installation teams across multiple live sites.

Health & Safety Manager

Aidan Loonie — Health & Safety Manager

Aidan Loonie is our Health & Safety Manager, ensuring that Pantera Wardrobes maintains the highest standards of health and safety across all sites. Aidan holds the NEBOSH General Certificate and is responsible for all H&S documentation, site inductions and compliance audits.

Karen Carroll

Office Manager

Karen Carroll — Office Manager

Karen Carroll is our Office Manager and the heartbeat of the Pantera Wardrobes office. Karen manages the day-to-day administration of the business, from coordinating deliveries to liaising with clients and ensuring everything runs smoothly.

Nina Cadogan

Accounts

Nina Cadogan — Accounts

Nina Cadogan manages our accounts function, handling all aspects of financial administration including invoicing, purchase ledger and payroll support. Nina ensures that our financial processes run efficiently and accurately.

What We Do

Pantera Wardrobes has been importing furniture from Italy since 2007. We work closely with Novamobili and Battistella to plan product manufacture and deliveries so that our products arrive on site exactly when our clients need them.

Our product ranges come from two Italian manufacturers, Novamobili and Battistella (cinquanta3), who share a factory in Treviso in the Veneto region of north-east Italy. Both companies have a philosophy of producing high quality, modular furniture that offers maximum flexibility in design and use.

We also offer Urbanmobili, our own UK-manufactured front-framed wardrobe system. Designed with the specification and style of Italian furniture, Urbanmobili bridges the gap between quality and affordability and is ideal for the developer market.

We work closely with our clients from pre-tender stage, through the planning and design process right through to installation. Our project management team will attend all pre-contract and progress meetings and will prepare fully detailed drawings and specifications for approval.

Our installation teams are fully employed by us, which means we can maintain the highest standards of workmanship and Health & Safety on site. Our CSCS-carded workforce complete SMSTS accredited site inductions and are fully trained in Manual Handling, Working at Height and other essential safety disciplines.

Our after-care service is equally important to us. We carry a comprehensive stock of spare parts and can respond quickly to any snagging or remedial works that may be required.

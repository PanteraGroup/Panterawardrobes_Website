Battistella — cinquanta3

'cinquanta3' is part of Battistella, a group established in 1953 and sister company to Novamobili. Battistella is one of Italy's leading manufacturers of furniture, producing high quality, modular wardrobe systems at their factory in Treviso, Veneto. The cinquanta3 range offers an impressive combination of quality, flexibility and value.

Hinged Wardrobes

cinquanta3 — Hinged Door System

The Battistella cinquanta3 hinged wardrobe system is a superb choice for residential developments requiring quality Italian furniture at a competitive price point. The clean, frameless door design suits a wide range of interior styles.

The system is available in a comprehensive range of exterior finishes including the ever-popular Bianco, Canapa, and a range of gloss and matt lacquers. Interior finishes include the refined Nuvola — a soft, textured grey-white tone.

Handle Options

cinquanta3 hinged doors are available with a range of handle options. The A70 and A76 handles are both popular choices for developer specifications, offering a clean and contemporary profile that complements the door finishes beautifully.

Handleless options with integrated finger-pull recesses are also available for a more minimalist aesthetic.

Interior Configuration

cinquanta3 wardrobes are supplied with fully fitted interiors as standard. Configurations can include full-height hanging, double hanging, adjustable shelving, and drawer units. All interior components are finished in the Nuvola interior as standard.

Sliding Wardrobes

Sliding Door System

The Battistella cinquanta3 sliding wardrobe system provides an elegant, space-efficient solution for bedroom wardrobes. The smooth-gliding door mechanism is engineered for quiet, reliable operation throughout the lifetime of the product.

Sliding doors are available in a range of finishes to complement the exterior wardrobe carcass. Mirror sliding doors are a particularly popular option for smaller rooms.

Finishes & Profiles

Sliding door finishes for the cinquanta3 range include Bianco, Canapa, Grey and Mirror options. Aluminium frame profiles are available in silver and other anodised finishes to coordinate with the door panels and hardware.

The top-hung track system keeps the floor clear and is easy to clean — an important consideration for residential developments.

Walk In Wardrobes

Walk In Wardrobe System

The Battistella cinquanta3 walk-in wardrobe system provides a luxury dressing room solution with the quality and flexibility you would expect from an Italian manufacturer. The modular system can be configured to suit any room layout and storage requirement.

Available in the same range of exterior and interior finishes as the hinged and sliding systems, the walk-in range allows consistent specification throughout a development. Integrated lighting options are available to complete the installation.

Nicaragua To Costa Rica – 390KM

Congratulations to Paul and Peter for completing the gruelling 390km cycle ride from Nicaragua to Costa Rica. Although the route was through some of the most beautiful scenery in Central America riding past smoking volcanoes, pristine beaches and around National Parks, the heat gradient and terrain ensured that this was no easy ride. A fantastic £17,205 was raised for Marie Curie and they agreed that this was their toughest ride ever!

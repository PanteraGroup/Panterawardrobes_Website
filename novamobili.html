Novamobili

Novamobili's philosophy is to provide high quality furniture which is modular and highly functional, offering maximum flexibility in design and use. Manufactured at their factory in Treviso in the Veneto region of north-east Italy, Novamobili wardrobes combine outstanding Italian craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Hinged Wardrobes

Alfa — Hinged Door System

The Novamobili Alfa hinged door system is one of our most popular ranges for residential developers. Available in an extensive range of finishes including matt lacquers, gloss lacquers and wood grains, the Alfa system offers outstanding versatility and quality.

The frameless door construction gives a seamless, contemporary appearance that works beautifully in both traditional and modern interiors. Soft-close hinges are fitted as standard.

Door Finishes

Novamobili offer an exceptional range of door finishes for the Alfa system, including the popular YNCA Bianco (white matt lacquer), Grigio and Tortora matt lacquers, and a range of wood grain and veneer finishes.

Handle options include the elegant Flap integrated handle, recessed bar handles and a range of standalone handle profiles in various finishes.

Internal Fittings

Novamobili internal fittings are engineered to maximise storage efficiency. Options include full-height hanging rails, adjustable shelving, drawer units, shoe racks and belt/tie racks. All fittings are available in matching interior finishes.

The Millerighe Pearla interior is a popular choice for residential developments, offering a sophisticated ribbed texture in a neutral pearl tone.

Catalogue

The full Novamobili hinged wardrobe catalogue is available to view online or by request. Our team can provide samples of all available finishes and handles to assist with specification.

Sliding Wardrobes

Miscela — Sliding Door System

The Novamobili Miscela sliding door system offers a sophisticated, space-saving solution for bedroom wardrobes. The aluminium frame profiles are available in Titanium, Gold and other finishes, with a wide range of infill panel options.

The smooth-running top-hung mechanism ensures effortless operation and the soft-close dampers provide a premium feel throughout the lifetime of the product.

Panel Options

Miscela sliding door panels are available in a wide range of finishes including mirror, glass, lacquer and wood grain. Panels can be mixed within a single door to create interesting design combinations.

Popular combinations include grey/canapa inserts with Titanium profiles, and mirror panels with white or dark wood surrounds.

Track & Profile Options

The Miscela track system is available in top-hung configuration, keeping the floor profile minimal and easy to clean. Aluminium track profiles are precision engineered for smooth, quiet operation and long-term durability.

Walk In Wardrobes

Wing — Walk In System

The Novamobili Wing walk-in wardrobe system transforms a dressing room space into a luxurious, highly functional storage solution. The modular system allows complete flexibility in layout and specification.

Wing is available in a range of finishes including Olmo Scuro (dark oak), Bianco and various lacquer options. Integrated lighting can be specified to enhance the appearance and functionality of the finished installation.

Internal Layout Options

Walk-in wardrobe layouts can be fully customised to suit the available space and the specific requirements of the development. Options include full-height hanging, double hanging, open shelving, drawers, shoe storage, trouser rails and pull-out accessories.

Lighting & Accessories

Novamobili offer a comprehensive range of integrated lighting solutions for walk-in wardrobes, including LED strip lighting under shelves, spotlights within hanging sections and illuminated mirror panels. Accessories such as valet rods, jewellery drawers and pull-out laundry baskets are also available.
